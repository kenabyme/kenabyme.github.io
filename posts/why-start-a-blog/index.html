Why Start a Blog?

Posted on Nov 2, 2023

I figure a good first post for a blog is an explanation of why the blog exists in the first place. Why do it? I want to start blogging for a simple reason: I want to write more often and I think it’s more fun to write where other people can read what you say. I hope that having this public space inspires me to do that.

What’s with the name?

ybane.me is a partially reversed version of en abyme, which is a phrase I borrowed from Mise en abyme which is an artistic technique of placing a copy of an image within itself in such a way that suggests infinite recursion. I’m reminded of this effect when I think about how my life involves a continual pursuit to live according to my own values while also satisyfing the expectations of the people I love. By resisting and satisfying those expecations, I both change and reinforce them. I’m a reflection of the people around me as much as they are a reflection of me. Much like standing between two mirrors that are facing one another, my values are always changing as a result of this push and pull, and the effects of this tension are infinite: they will continue for the rest of my life.
